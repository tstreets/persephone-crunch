The Bear Lake Monster
The Bear Lake Monster story originated in 19th-century, mainly being spread through articles, written by Joseph C. Rich, a settler from the Latter-day Saints community.This cryptid is descirbed in multiple different ways, examples being that it is a long, serpent-like creature, measuring roughly 40 to 50 feet, with its limbs being around 18 inches long. Its head has been described as as being similar to a cow, otter, crocodile, and walrus. Spikes are also seen on its tail. In the past, most sightings were beilieved, forming a huge interest in this cryptid, but in recent days, these stories are often met with skepticism from various newspapers.
